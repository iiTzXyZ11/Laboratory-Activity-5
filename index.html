Early Life

I was born in Ozamiz City but grew up moving between several cities, including Cebu, Iligan, and back to Ozamiz. I have always been fascinated by computers. Even as a child, I was more interested in understanding how they worked than in playing the games they offered. This early curiosity led me to explore technology in every way I could, tinkering with gadgets and attempting to fix devices—even when I had little knowledge of how they functioned. My natural inclination to experiment and problem-solve from a young age sparked a lifelong interest in technology and the skills needed to navigate it.
